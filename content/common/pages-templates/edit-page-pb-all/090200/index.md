# Edit a Page via the Persona Bar

## Steps

1.  Go to the page to edit.
2.  Go to Persona Bar \> Edit.
    
    ![Persona Bar > Edit](img/scr-pbar-all-Edit-E91.png)
    
3.  Add, configure, edit, delete, or move the modules on the page.
